About me

Splita

Split large CSV or JSON files into smaller downloadable chunks

Role:Research implementation, documentation,low fidelity wireframes, high fidelity wireframes

Splita is a website that splits large CSV and JSON files into smaller sizes for free. It allows users to upload either a CSV or JSON files and lets them choose their own chunk sizes and later save the smaller sizes either on their local storage or any other storage platform they choose. Whilst a lot of people don't mind compressing large files, most cry about the poor quality of compressed files. Splitta offers users an opportunity to go around that and reduce the heavy load that is presented on processes by large CSV and JSON files.

Client

I4G X Zuri training

Duration

4 weeks

Problem statement

To create a platform that lets users split large CSV or JSON files into smaller sizes called chunks.

Team goals

To design and develop a free, fully functional and responsive website that will allow users to chunk large CSV or JSON files into smaller sizes whilst they choose their own sizes and allow them to save and download them immediately or after some time. The website will allow unauthenticated users to only browse the website and view the documentation while only authenticated users will get access to the dashboard and split their files.

Scope

This was not a very large project and since we were a group of designers we were able to finish it in the stipulated time. The design work throughout the project was distributed well and effectively and GitHub issues and Trello were used to assign and manage tasks and coupled with regular google meet meetings we progressed well. Tasks were decided in this format: User experience and interface design User research and implementation Brainstorming Information architecture Design system development Wireframing and prototyping Designing for responsivity Iteration Web development Database schema Choosing Framework Front-end and back-end development Testing Deployment on Heroku The entire process was however linear and both designers and developers worked continuously to create the best product.

User Research

I was responsible for the user research implementation. From a previous survey form that let us narrow down potential users, I was able to set up interviews with potential users. With the research plan as a guide I was able to conduct over 60% of video and call interviews. The entire process took less than a week and the interview results were compiled and sent to the team members that were responsible for user journey mapping. I chose . do research implementation because I believed whilst I was good at ux writing, my research skills were rather not satisfactory so it helped me improve in conducting and carrying out effective and meaningful interviews.. The three main research methods used in this project are surveys ,interview,Observation and secondary research.
